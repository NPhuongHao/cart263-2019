A card game based on the gameplay of Spoopy Squad's Underhand.

In this game, you play as the mayor of a small village near the forest. Pressured by wars and the wolfs nearby, the mayor signed a contract with the Big Wolf, in order to minimize the damage. Each 30 weeks, the Big Wolf will come ask for its due.

Manage your resources and survive through the weeks, and maybe you'll be able to break the contract!
